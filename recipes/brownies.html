Ingredients

  • 2 eggs and water
  • Powdered sugar
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Oil
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix the dry and wet ingredients into two separate bowls.
  2. Combine the wet and dry ingredients.
  3. Pour the batter into an 8x8 inch baking pan lined with parchment paper
  4. Bake for 40-45 minutes at 325 degrees.
